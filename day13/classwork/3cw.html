The Tesla Model 3 has dimensions of approximately 4,720 mm in length, 1,850 mm in width, and 1,443 mm in height. The Tesla Model 3's maximum towing capacity is 1,000kg.

The Standard Tesla Model 3 comes with rear-wheel drive, and on top of the huge 15.4-inch touchscreen that dominates the Tesla's interior, the Model 3 comes as standard with power-adjustable and heated front seats, Bluetooth connectivity and a heated steering wheel.
